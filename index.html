About Us

Overview


To Bring People To Learn About Mental Health


HealthCode is a Mental Health Advocacy and Peer Support (NGO) NGO based in Selangor. It was founded in September 2019 and registered under ROS with the registration number PPM-027-10-14092017.

We focus on promoting awareness on the importance of mental health as well as offer online calling service for mental health issues or conditions victims such as sharing session and therapy session.

We exists as an informative online platform on mental health but also spiritually being a key component, highlighting the critical importance of mental health recovery model on empowerment, autonomy and right-based approach.

Vision & Mission

V I S I O N


"To be the leading voice in mental health education in support of mental illness patients"


M I S S I O N


  • To promote awareness on the importance of a good mental health.
  • To raise awareness on mental health issues.
  • To provide support for mental illness patients and carers.
  • To promote mental illness literacy.
  • To support other bodies in all areas to promote mental health.

Services

How Can We Help?


The World Health Organization (WHO) projects that 1 million people will die by suicide this year — 1 death every 40 seconds around the world.
HealthCode aims to reduce the number of suicide rate due to mental health condtions.

We've build an online call support team that help patients with mental health conditions during an emergency such as meltdown, stress out, panic attacks and many more.

This particular team are gathered by experts from all around the world with the same ambition - to help people deal with their mental health condition in the most accepting way.


Join The Community

Mental Health Community


We’re social beings, and we are not meant to live in isolation. Community is critical for us to thrive, especially for someone with mental illness who is already experiencing the common symptoms of loneliness and isolation.

Community provides many elements that are critical to mental health.
We have created a community or in other words, a "SAFE SPACE" for all people to allow them to share their experiences with mental health conditions as well as empowering them to help themselves and supporting others.
