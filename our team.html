OUR TEAM


Our layered care approach provided by a team of experienced and fully qualified
mental health professionals means we can deliver the highest quality care and support.

He has over 25 years of clinical and consultancy experience across a variety of settings including inpatient and out outpatient clinics, crisis intervention, structured outpatient programs, inpatient psychiatric, and private practice. OUR SPEAKERS


They experienced what you are experiencing and they are sharing their stories.

Daiyan Trisha
Musician

Her song Kita Manusia was written with her own mental health struggles as the backbone of it. The songstress wanted to highlight mental illnesses such as depression, anxiety, depersonalisation and self-harm. She opened up about her struggles with work and family as well as pointed out that the struggles are normal and can be experienced by anyone. As humans, everyone is bound to face difficulties and therefore, there is no need to hide those feelings.

Arwind Kumar
Youtuber

He has been using his fame as a platform to create awareness and discuss serious topics that teens face today, all with a touch of light-hearted humour. After being bullied in school, Arwind became determined to bring these issues to light and this year returned as the StandTogether Kindness Ambassador, in support of National Kindness Week for the second year now. He stand by the words, "Every stories matter to me and you are never alone".

Rose E. Daughton
Influencer

"Talking about it dramatically changed my life so never feel ashamed!". Over the years, she has recorded her experience with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), offering her accounts of this commonly misunderstood condition that can cause instability in one's relationships and damage self-perceptions. She is straightforward about her disorder and she has admitted that it has dramatically impacted her relationships. Now, she wants to share her stories with you.
