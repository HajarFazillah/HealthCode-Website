Testimonials


This is what they have to say.

Aryan

When you’re desperate to remain invisible, you begin to think that anything that could go wrong, will go wrong. It wasn’t until after leaving school, that I realised that these fears hadn’t subsided. Once I realised exactly what was going on with my mind, and could put a label on it, I began opening up to my friends and family about how I was feeling. HealthCode is so supportive and non-judgmental, I know I would ever have been able to achieve any of these changes without their help.

Asyiqin

I was diagnosed with OCD when I was younger and has always been struggling. However, receiving help just made me a better person than yesterday. In a short space of time this all helped my sense of self-awareness, self-confidence and ultimately made me feel like I could take back control. HealthCode helped me a lot and this comes from a place of heartfelt integrity and supreme professionalism. I'm so honored to be part of their essential contribution to health and healing.

Mia

I would recommend therapy to anyone and I do, but I always say it depends on the therapist. I’ve been seeing my therapist for two years now and the progress I have made is astonishing. Therapy has given me the tools to fight my depression and anxiety in a healthy way. I no longer feel broken and if and when I do, I have a strong support system behind me. It’s affected how I view life and has given me a second chance. I’m a better and healthier person because of it.
